The Fitzies Europe Tour 2002

Well we're home! And what a fabulous excursion it was!

In the course of our journey, Maureen's web page has grown dramatically. What started out as a simple 1-page itinerary with the aim of letting friends and family track our progress, has become a significant travel diary, complete with pictures which had to be downloaded each day from the digital camera.

We've broken the Travellers' Diary up into separate pages to make it faster to download for those with dial-up internet connections. Click on the underlined links below to go to the pages with pictures of what we saw. Some, but not all, of the pictures can be enlarged by clicking on them.
