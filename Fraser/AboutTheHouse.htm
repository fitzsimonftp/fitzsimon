Fraser Island Luxury Beach House

This is the perfect holiday home for a family or two couples, with or without children.

The deck overlooks the pool area making it the perfect place to relax while keeping an eye on the youngsters.  The deck is protected by a child-proof pool gate which is also dingo-proof.

The layout of the house is typical of a "Queenslander", with rooms opening off to either side of a central breezeway.

Both Bedroom 1 and Bedroom 2 are equipped with Queen size beds. Bedroom 1 has a full ensuite while Bedroom 2 has private access to the main Bathroom.

Bedroom 3 contains two single beds, one of which is equipped with a roll-out trundle to accommodate a third person.

The large "L"-shaped lounge suite incorporates a fold-out double bed.  (Speak to the managers about the suitability of this for your group.)

See also photos of the Fraser Island Luxury Beach House.

The house is available in two configurations:

  • 3 Bedrooms (2 Bathrooms, accommodates 6 guests), or

  • 2 Bedrooms (1 Bathroom, accommodates 4 guests, excludes Bedroom 1)

Features

  • Linen: All linen, sheets, towels, blankets are provided.

  • Kitchen: The fully-equipped kitchen features cutlery, crockery, glassware, a dishwasher, refrigerator, oven, stove, microwave and other appliances.

  • Other facilities include a washing machine, clothes dryer, telephone, CD/DVD player and Austar satellite TV.

    Please be aware that Fraser Island is a remote wilderness area.  Should an appliance break down, the on-site managers will do everything possible to effect a speedy repair.  However, sometimes this just cannot be done immediately if it requires parts or tradespeople from the mainland.  Please bring your sense of adventure to the island.
