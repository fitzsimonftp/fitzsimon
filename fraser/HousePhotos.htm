Fraser Island Luxury Beach House Photos

This page contains photos of various rooms of the Fraser Island Luxury Beach House. Please wait while they load.
 

The house is Number 19 in a managed resort of 20 houses, trading as Fraser Island Beach Houses.  This is located at Eurong Second Valley and is accessed directly from the beach, 200m south of the longer-established Eurong Beach Resort.

That's House 19 with the 4WD in the carport. The pool is on the other side of the house.

This is the view from the resort's whale-watching platform.

The deck overlooks the pool and BBQ area and is protected by a child-proof pool gate which is also dingo-proof.

Both Bedroom 1 and Bedroom 2 are equipped with Queen size beds.

Bedroom 1 has a full ensuite.
 

Bedroom 2 has private access to the main Bathroom.
 

Bedroom 3 contains two single beds, one of which is equipped with a roll-out trundle to accommodate a third person.

 

The large "L"-shaped lounge suite incorporates a fold-out double bed. (Speak to the on-site managers about whether this is suitable for your group.)
 

The fully-equipped kitchen features a dishwasher, refrigerator, oven, stove, microwave and other appliances.


Other facilities include:

  • washing machine,
  • clothes dryer,
  • telephone,
  • CD/DVD player and
  • Austar satellite TV.

A wireless internet connection is also available.
