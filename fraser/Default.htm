Fraser Island Luxury Beach House

Fully equipped and serviced Luxury Holiday Accommodation on the beach on the Eastern (surf) side of the largest sand island in the world, Queensland's Fraser Island.

Experience everything that this World Heritage-listed island has to offer: giant sand blows, towering rainforests, rare flora and fauna, sparkling freshwater lakes and creeks...

...then come home to a great night's rest.
"We hope you enjoy your stay in our house and come to love Fraser Island as much as we do." 
      - Mike, Maureen, Peter, Andrew & Robbie (Owners)
